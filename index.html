Bramble Trail

An outdoor scavenger hunt for curious kids

Go outside and
find the wild things.

A gentle scavenger hunt for children, set in the woods, parks, ponds and hedgerows on your doorstep. Pick a trail, head outside, and see what you can discover together.

How it works

Pick a trail

Choose a habitat and how long you have. The app sets a little list of things to find.

Head outside

Explore together and tick off each find as you spot it. No screens to stare at, just the world.

Collect finds

Build a field journal of everything found and earn badges along the way.

Woods and meadows, parks and ponds, hedgerows and the wild edges in between. There is always something to find.
